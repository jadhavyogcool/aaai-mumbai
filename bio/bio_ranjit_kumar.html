Biography

Dr. Ranjit Kumar is an Assistant Professor at Ajeenkya DY Patil University, Pune, specializing in Biotechnology, Bioinformatics, and Drug Designing. He completed his M.Tech from Pune University, worked as a Research Associate at IIT Bombay, and earned his Ph.D. from ICMR-NARI, Pune. He serves as Faculty In-Charge of the Research and Innovation Committee and holds leadership roles in IEEE, including Young Professionals Chair, IEEE Maharashtra Section, and Secretary of the IEEE Maharashtra Section Affinity Group (CN-Nagpur). He has published 35 Scopus-indexed papers, holds 16 patents, authored 3 books with 9 chapters, and is a three-time Young Researcher Award recipient.
