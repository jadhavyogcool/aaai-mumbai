Biography

Dr. Pramod Bide, He has completed his B.E, M.E and Ph.D degree in Computer Engineering in 2011, 2014 and 2024 respectively from University of Mumbai, India. He is a an Associate Dean- Administration and Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Engineering, Sardar Patel Institute of Technology, University of Mumbai. He has 14 years of experience in Academic and Research. His research interests include Social Network Mining,Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, Data Mining, Data Analytics. He has published 3 papers in peer reviewed journals and 50 + papers in international conferences. He has been invited to more than 50+ colleges to deliver technical talk and Presentations at International Conferences, Workshops, Seminars and AICTE-ISTE Short Term Training Programs (STTP) at several places in India. He has delivered 77+ expert talks on various emerging topics across various Institutes and Universities in India. He has received two best research paper awards in the year 2020 and 2023. He has received funding from IEEE Computer Society and Mumbai University.He has received best volunteer award for standing committee of the IEEE Bombay Section 2023. He has organized 15+ workshops and FDP's. He has attended 40+ workshops and FDP's. He has received grant from IEEE CS Rupees 320000/- and Grant of 20 lakh Rupees from DST , MeiTY and EICT for conducting FDP. He has reviewed over 100+ research papers for reputed journals and international conferences. Dr. Bide is an AICTE–CDAC certified Master Trainer in High Performance Computing and Artificial Intelligence and DST CDAC certified Quantum Computing Trainer.He has successfully completed a consultancy of Rupees 60000 for NIRRH. Demonstrating a strong commitment to research and innovation,Dr. Bide has submitted more than 15 research and project proposals to prestigious funding and professional bodies such as the Department of Science and Technology (DST), MeitY, NSM, various Government of India ministries, and IEEE at the global level, with a cumulative proposed funding of nearly ₹4 crore.He has successfully secured ₹15 lakhs in sponsorship funding for ISEC 2027, supported by ACM India and leading industry partners. He is an IEEE Senior member, member of Computer Society of India and Life member of Indian Society for Technical Education. And Senior Member of Indian National Academy of Engineering.
