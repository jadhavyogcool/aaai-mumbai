Biography

Dr. Deepa Parasar is a Professor in the Computer Science & Engineering Department with over 21 years of teaching experience. She is currently associated with Amity University, Mumbai, Her research interests include machine learning, image processing, artificial intelligence, and cloud computing. She has guided several Ph.D., M. Tech., and undergraduate projects and has published numerous research papers in reputed journals and conferences. She is also a recipient of the DAAD AI-Net Fellowship from DAAD, Germany, and participated in the international networking tour to Germany in 2022. Dr. Parasar has received several awards for academic excellence and contributions to research and teaching and has also been granted 2 patents in AI and image analysis. She actively contributes to research, academic leadership, and international collaborations.
